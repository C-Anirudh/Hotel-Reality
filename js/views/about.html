About

Hotel Reality is an immersive app experience which illustrates the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) of a hotel in dimension of augmenting imagination on real world.
The app includes the following two features which display:
  • Virtual Hotel Mascot object to the hotel customers using the app for entertainment purposes
  • Real-time indicators of property/hub like:
    • Average Daily Rate
    • Revenue per Available Room
    • Average Length Of Stay
    • Occupancy Rate
    • Online Rating
    • RevPAR Room Type Index
    • Market Penetration Index
    • Total Available Rooms
    • Energy Mangement KPI like Electricity, Water, Gas, etc.
for Customer Representatives (Front Desk officer), Hotel Managers, Head of Back Office, Director/Head of the Management, Board of Directors (need to validate), and Owner - Managing Directors (MD) for making conscious decisions on hotel performance in a given period with virtual hotel object on the real-world objects using Augmented Reality platform.
